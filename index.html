About

Architect practicing in New York City with work spanning institutional, residential, senior living, and healthcare projects—from single-family residences to developments exceeding 900,000 square feet.

Currently at Perkins Eastman, a global architecture firm recognized for design excellence in healthcare, senior living, and large-scale mixed-use developments. Previously at goCstudio in Seattle, an award-winning residential studio known for crafted modern homes in the Pacific Northwest.

Parallel to practice, pursues independent design research through international competitions, exploring emerging questions in mass timber high-rise construction, health-centered housing for underserved communities, and adaptive preservation of historic structures.

Project work featured in ArchDaily, Dezeen, New York Times, Wall Street Journal, and Architectural Record.
